Campus Tree Planting Initiative

EarthBound Co. hosts volunteer planting days to help restore green spaces on campus and around Troy to celebrate Earth Day. We work in areas like Washington Park and the Troy University Arboretum, depending on the project. Our next event will take place at Washington Park on April 22, 2026, at 9:00 a.m. Additional dates for March, April, September, and October will be posted as our schedule is confirmed.

Environmental Awareness Workshop

EarthBound Co. will be visiting Charles Henderson Middle School during Earth Week to talk with students about simple, everyday ways to help the environment. We’ll share tips on recycling, reducing waste, and taking care of local green spaces. This workshop is scheduled for Thursday, April 23, 2026, at 2:00 p.m. We are still looking for volunteers as this will be a hands-on workshop for multiple classrooms. We hope to add more outreach days as other schools show interest.

Neighborhood Shade Days

EarthBound Co. values community and nature, and so we love to give back to both. We organize weekend “Shade Days” in specific neighborhoods where volunteers plant street and yard trees to reduce heat, improve air quality, and beautify streets. Partner with local councils or neighborhood associations to identify high-need blocks and have residents “adopt” nearby trees for watering and basic care.

Memorial & Celebration Groves

Earthbound Co. understands the importance of family. Which is why we offer families and organizations the chance to sponsor trees planted in “Remembrance & Celebration Groves” to honor loved ones, milestone events, or community heroes. Host a short annual ceremony or open day at each grove where people can visit, learn about the species planted, and see the collective impact.
