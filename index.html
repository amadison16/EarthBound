Who We Are

EarthBound Co. is a student-run initiative focused on reforestation, environmental education, and sustainability outreach. Whether planting trees or partnering with local communities, our goal is to help the planet one project at a time.

What We Do

  • Organize local tree-planting events
  • Promote environmental awareness
  • Support conservation-based nonprofits
