About Us

Earth Bound is a student led initiative dedicated to restoring balance between people and the planet through reforestation, environmental education, and sustainability outreach. We believe meaningful change begins locally whether it’s planting trees, organizing workshops, or collaborating with community partners to promote greener practices. Every project we take on reflects our commitment to protecting natural ecosystems, empowering others to take action, and building a more sustainable future for all.
